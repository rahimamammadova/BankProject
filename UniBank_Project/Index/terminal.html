Deposit cells

Going away on leave or leaving on a business trip? Out of home often and for long? Concerned about custody of your property? We offer you the rent of deposit cells of different sizes and for unlimited time.

Main Conditions:

You can store anything in those cells, be it money, securities, paperwork and jewelry, antiques or simply those things that are important to you (*apart from everything banned by the law)

Right now, you can use our deposit cells at Head office, Yasamal branch, Nefchiler branch, branch no 16

We assume the fullest responsibility for preservation of your property. We also guarantee complete anonymity

* This service is provided only to residents.
