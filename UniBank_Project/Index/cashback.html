Cashback & Miles

Up to 30% cashback on every purchase.

When paying with Unibank cards via Apple Pay/Google Pay in all cafes and restaurants in the world, all petrol stations in the world (maximum 20 AZN per month), all markets and online markets in the world ( With payments in the market, you can earn a maximum of 10 AZN at a time and maximum of 20 AZN per month ) you can earn 5% cashback.

Cashback is available for all Unibank cards (except for UniMiles, Mastercard Black Edition, Mastercard World Elite and Visa Infinite cards). İnstead of cashback, you earn miles on these cards.

You can get additional information about the Miles bonuses on the UniMiles page.
